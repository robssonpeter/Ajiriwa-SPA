About the Project

Ajiriwa is a revolutionary recruitment platform designed to simplify the job-seeking and hiring process in Tanzania. Our mission is to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers, making it easier for both parties to connect and succeed.

Our Technology Stack

  • Front End: Vue.js for smooth user interfaces
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS for beautiful and responsive designs
  • Back End: Laravel framework powered by PHP
  • Database: MySQL for secure data storage

Our Challenge

In Tanzania, job seekers often struggle to create professional profiles to land a job. Ajiriwa is here to guide candidates step by step, helping them create impressive profiles and generating professional CVs. For employers, we offer a sophisticated tool to manage job applications effectively, streamlining the hiring process.

What We Offer

  1. Candidate Section: Job seekers can create professional profiles and generate CVs effortlessly.
  2. Employers Section: Companies can create profiles, customize appearances, and post job adverts with ease.
  3. Administration Section: Admins can manage the platform and create job advertisements on behalf of companies.

Our Vision

We aim to simplify recruitment procedures in Tanzania. While there are a few existing solutions, Ajiriwa stands out by offering advanced tools to streamline hiring. Employers can browse candidates based on experience, skills, and more, making the hiring process efficient and effective.

My Journey as a Developer

My journey as a developer started with a passion and interest in computing and programming. I was fortunate to get the opportunity to work at DHL Supply Chain company in Turkey as an intern in the Human Resources department. It was there that I witnessed how they handled recruitment processes using well-designed systems, and this sparked my interest in embarking on this incredible journey.

I began my coding journey from scratch, driven by a desire to make a difference. I created the basic version of Ajiriwa, which, although functional, was susceptible to hacking and other vulnerabilities.

Then, I discovered the Alx Software Engineering Program, and without hesitation, I applied. Today, I am a much-improved developer, armed with the knowledge and skills to rework the Ajiriwa recruitment project, making it modern and secure.

My journey is a testament to the power of determination and continuous learning. I am excited to share this journey with you as we strive to make Ajiriwa a reliable and innovative platform for both job seekers and employers.
