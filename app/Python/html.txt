<h2>Our Story</h2>
<p>Comprehensive Community Based Rehabilitation in Tanzania (CCBRT) aims to be the preferred provider of accessible specialized health services in Africa and serves as healthcare social enterprise and through development programmes in the community and the most vulnerable. Committed to preventing lifelong disabilities wherever possible, CCBRT is also engaged in extensive maternal and newborn healthcare (MNHC) activities including obstetric fistula repairs. Its Maternity and Newborn Wing serves as a referral centre for high risk and emergency deliveries in the region of Dar es Salaam and the Eastern Zone of Tanzania.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>In order to strengthen the Human Resource Department, we are looking for an<strong>&nbsp;energetic, analytical HR Professional</strong>&nbsp;who will assist in the provision of all HR services towards our business units.</em></p>
<div class="code-block code-block-center code-block-2">&nbsp;</div>
<h2>Your role</h2>
<p>Under the guidance and direct supervision of the Head of Human Resources, the junior Human Resources Business Partner (HRBP) is responsible for the effective delivery of HR services using the HR Business Partnering approach. You will help to assess client needs, interpret and apply the CCBRT HR strategy and policies, rules and regulations, contribute to establishing internal procedures and providing solutions to a wide spectrum of complex HR issues. As HR Business Partner, you deliver a value-added service to line managers and employees by working in close collaboration with the line managers on all staff management issues.</p>
<p>On a day-to-day basis, you will be responsible for all HR matters for allocated departments that you serve as HRBP.</p>
<div class="code-block code-block-center code-block-2">&nbsp;</div>
<p>This include all HR administration (compliance matters, leave), Performance Management, Recruitment &amp; Selection, Training Needs assessment, Disciplinary support etc. As part of the HR team, you also will assist in the payroll administration and HRIS implementation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
